How to find Me

Jet Set Corvette is a cozy single-chair studio boutique salon where you get full attention. It's located in North Salem inside the Cedar Village retirement center. Go into the main entrance and use the curved stairs and at the top of the stairs take a right. Continue walking and the Salon is located down the first small hallway to your right. We are also wheelchair accessible! Wheelchair access is through an elevator, which is directly outside the Salon hallway.
